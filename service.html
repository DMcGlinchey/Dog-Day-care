Dog walking

Dog walking sessions are available every day between the hours of 7am to 7pm. These sessions can be single dog walks or as a group. Price will differ depending on the session and the dog breed. Walks will be done to best meet the dog needs, these walks will differ depending on the dogs breed, age and gender. Single walks are highly demanded can fill up quickly, we advise dog walk in group as it creates a happier environment for them. Dogs who walk in group need evidence that the animal has been neutered and get along with other dogs. (Service may not be available over holidays as Christmas and Easter)



House sitting

House sitting sessions are available every day, at an hourly time ranges between 1 to 12 hours, further time can be discussed before appointment. Price will differ depending on the number of hours spent with the dog. These sessions are when member of staff come around to your house and look after your pet. Session as these can be useful for anxious who don’t like being in new environment, may prove useful for dog who don’t do well with others. (Service may not be available over holidays as Christmas and Easter)



Day Care

Day care sessions are available everyday between the hours of 7am to 7pm. These sessions will require a dog to behave around others, including dogs and staff. Dog will also be required to be neutered, as dogs will be interacting with one another. In day care, dog will be entertained and taken for walks, dogs may also be feed if owners request it. Currently there is no option for care to individual dogs. This service may not prove useful for dog who are anxious and struggle to be away from their owner. Future hope to allow single session care for dogs, this will help with anxious dogs and unneutered dogs.



Dog board

Dog boarding are sessions that take place over night. Useful for owner who are heading on holiday or require last minute care. Care is between 1 to 7-night, further stay can be done on request. Boarding session will provide a bed, food and walk if need be. Dogs that are stay for multiple day will receive that same treatment as dog day-care sessions. Dog will also be required to be neutered, as dogs will be interacting with others. Currently there is no option for care to individual dog. This service may not prove useful for dog who are anxious and struggle to be away from their owner. Future hope to allow single session care for dogs, this will help with anxious dogs and unneutered dogs.
