About Us


The goal is to cater to canine care, for dogs while owners are at work or on long trips. After having bad experiences with past dog kennel, having our dogs return home scared and even one occasion with flees. We want to create a company dogs feel safe and allows cus-tomers to feel confident in sending them. Currently working a 9 to 5 job is required, if you want to make a living. Leaving people, no time in the middle of the day to look after their pets. In the past, couple may have one of them stay home all day looking after their pets. But now it's normal for both partners to work a full-time job. We fully understand this and want to provide all the support we can. Provide service that best meet you and help you take care of your furry companion.
